How to Keep Psychologically Healthy?

manage these problems, they are likely to cause various mental illnesses.

There are a lot of ways to curb mental health problems and keep psychologically healthy. Firstly, find the real
cause of your mental health problem and see whether you can do something about it. Secondly, learn to relax

friend about your problem, or seek support and advice form a psychological consultant.

While others, including most old people as well as scholars, value libraries. They love libraries because they
can enjoy peace and quietness in an isolated spiritual world. And they also have access to rare academic works
needed for their studies in the libraries.

In my view, conventional libraries are still available though there is still much room for improving. At the age
of information, as long as knowledge is respected and cherished, libraries wonâ€™t extinct from our lives. On the
other hand, libraries should not refuse the latest information technology, which will enable them to regain those

If asked whether the Internet is the greatest discovery of the 20th century, my answer would be â€œyesâ€. The
